**Bade'e Al Oud Amethyst** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Oriental Vanilla fragrance for women and men. **Bade'e Al Oud Amethyst** was launched in 2021. Top notes are Pink Pepper and Bergamot; middle notes are Turkish Rose, Bulgarian Rose and Jasmine; base notes are Agarwood (Oud), Amber and Vanilla.
