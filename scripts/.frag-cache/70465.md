**Fakhar Black** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Oriental fragrance for men. **Fakhar Black** was launched in 2022. Top notes are Apple, Bergamot and Ginger; middle notes are Lavender, Sage, Juniper Berries and Geranium; base notes are Tonka Bean, Cedar, Amberwood and Vetiver.
