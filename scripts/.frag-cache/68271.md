**Supremacy Not Only Intense** by **Afnan** is a Oriental Spicy fragrance for men. **Supremacy Not Only Intense** was launched in 2021. The nose behind this fragrance is Imran Fazlani. Top notes are Black Currant, Bergamot and Apple; middle notes are Oakmoss, Patchouli and Lavender; base notes are Ambergris, Musk and Saffron.
