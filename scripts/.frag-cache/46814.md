**Oud Mood** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a fragrance for women and men. Top notes are Rose, Saffron and Pimento; middle notes are Agarwood (Oud), Caramel, Floral Notes and Patchouli; base notes are Woody Notes, Amber, Resins, Incense and Musk.
