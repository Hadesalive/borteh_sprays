**Asad Zanzibar** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a fragrance for men. **Asad Zanzibar** was launched in 2024. The nose behind this fragrance is Fanny Bal. Top notes are Lavender and Black Pepper; middle notes are Coconut Water, Iris and Salt; base notes are Vanilla and Incense.
