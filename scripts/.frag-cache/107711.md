**Barakkat Satin Oud** by **Fragrance World** is a Oriental Woody fragrance for women and men. **Barakkat Satin Oud** was launched in 2024. Top note is Violet; middle notes are Bulgarian Rose, Turkish Rose and Laotian Oud; base notes are Agarwood, Vanilla, Amber and Benzoin.

Barakkat Satin Oud by Fragrance World is an oriental woody fragrance designed for both men and women. It features a complex blend of violet top notes, a heart of Laotian oud paired with Bulgarian and Turkish roses, and a warm base of vanilla, amber, benzoin, and agarwood. The scent is described as sensual, opulent, and long-lasting with a luxurious powdery drydown. Inspired by Maison Francis Kurkdjian's Oud Satin Mood, it maintains a balance between intense oud character and refined sweetness.
