**Junoon** by **Al Haramain Perfumes** is a Floral fragrance for women. **Junoon** was launched in 2016. The nose behind this fragrance is Christian Carbonnel. Top notes are Powdery Notes, Rose, Ylang-Ylang and Jasmine; middle notes are Iris, Tonka Bean, Rose and Jasmine; base notes are Heliotrope, Musk and Vanilla.
