**Khamrah Qahwa** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Oriental Vanilla fragrance for women and men. **Khamrah Qahwa** was launched in 2023. Top notes are Cinnamon, Cardamom and Ginger; middle notes are Praline, Candied Fruits and White Flowers; base notes are Vanilla, Coffee, Tonka Bean, Benzoin and Musk.
