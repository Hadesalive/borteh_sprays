**Musk Malaki** by **Swiss Arabian** is a fragrance for women and men. Top note is Lily-of-the-Valley; middle note is White Musk; base notes are Musk and Ambergris.

"Capture the magic of the Arabian desert on a moonlit night. A bewitching silver harmony of flowers, amber and musk like a fragrant oasis under a starry sky. Ultra-premium, ultra-luxurious – Musk Malaki." - a note from the brand.
