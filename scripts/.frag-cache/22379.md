**Royale Blue** by **Rasasi** is a Woody Floral Musk fragrance for men. Top notes are Sea Notes, Bergamot, Melon and Galbanum; middle notes are Lavender, Violet, Jasmine and Rose; base notes are Musk, Sandalwood, Cedar, Amber and Patchouli.
