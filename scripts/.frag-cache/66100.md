**Amber Oud Rouge** by **Al Haramain Perfumes** is a Oriental Floral fragrance for women and men. **Amber Oud Rouge** was launched in 2019. Top note is Saffron; middle note is Jasmine; base notes are Ambergris, Musk and White Cedar Extract.
