**Shaghaf Oud** by **Swiss Arabian** is a Oriental fragrance for women and men. Top note is Saffron; middle notes are Agarwood (Oud) and Rose; base notes are Agarwood (Oud), Praline and Vanilla.

"A captivating dance of Oriental opulence with French elegance, Shaghaf Oud is an ardent, fiery mix that holds nothing back." - a note from the brand.
