**Oud Romancea** by **Ard Al Zaafaran** is a Oriental Woody fragrance for women. Top notes are Mandarin Orange, Black Currant and Bergamot; middle notes are Spicy Notes, Cinnamon, Gardenia, Ylang-Ylang and Jasmine; base notes are Agarwood (Oud), Incense, Sandalwood, Amber and Patchouli.

"Oud Romancea starts slightly fresh with bergamot, mandarins and blackcurrant. The warm and floral heart note contains cinnamon, spicy notes, jasmine, gardenias and ylang ylang. The base is woody and earthy warm with amber, incense, sandalwood, Oudh and patchouli. A beautiful oriental perfume that combines beautiful floral warmth with woody and oriental notes." - a note from the brand.
