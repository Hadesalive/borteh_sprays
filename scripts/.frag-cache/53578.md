**Shuhrah Pour Homme** by **Rasasi** is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for men. Top notes are Tomato Leaf, Rose and Freesia; middle notes are Rose, Sandalwood, Cedar and Jasmine; base notes are Leather, Agarwood (Oud), Musk, Oakmoss and Amber.
