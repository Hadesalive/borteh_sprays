**Club de Nuit Woman** by **Armaf** is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women. Top notes are Orange, Bergamot, Grapefruit and Peach; middle notes are Rose, Jasmine, Litchi and Geranium; base notes are Patchouli, Musk, Vanilla and Vetiver.
