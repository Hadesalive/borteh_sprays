**Teriaq** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a fragrance for women and men. **Teriaq** was launched in 2024. The nose behind this fragrance is Quentin Bisch. Top notes are Caramel, Bitter Almond, Apricot and Pink Pepper; middle notes are Honey, Rhubarb, White Flowers and Rose; base notes are Leather, Vanilla, Musk, Labdanum and Vetiver.
