**Club de Nuit Sillage** by **Armaf** is a Floral Woody Musk fragrance for women and men. **Club de Nuit Sillage** was launched in 2020. Top notes are Bergamot, Lemon, Lime, Black Currant, Violet Leaf and Ginger; middle notes are Rose, Jasmine and Iris; base notes are Ambroxan, Musk, Sandalwood and Cedar.
