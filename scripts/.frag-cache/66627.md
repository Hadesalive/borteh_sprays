**Rare Carbon** by **Afnan** is a Leather fragrance for men. **Rare Carbon** was launched in 2020. Top notes are Leather, Violet Leaf, Nutmeg and Cinnamon; middle notes are Violet, Agarwood (Oud), Rose and Cedar; base notes are Vetiver, Sandalwood and Amber.
