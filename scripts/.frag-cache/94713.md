**Liquid Brun** by **French Avenue** is a Woody fragrance for men. **Liquid Brun** was launched in 2024. Top notes are Cinnamon, Orange Blossom, Cardamom and Bergamot; middle notes are Bourbon Vanilla and elemi; base notes are Praline, Ambroxan, Guaiac Wood and Musk.
