**Amber Oud Gold Edition** by **Al Haramain Perfumes** is a Oriental Vanilla fragrance for women and men. **Amber Oud Gold Edition** was launched in 2024. Top notes are Bergamot and Green Notes; middle notes are Melon, Pineapple, Amber and Gourmand Accord; base notes are Vanilla, Musk and Woody Notes.

Haramain Amber Oud Gold Edition is an opulent fragrance masterpiece that perfectly balances the timeless allure of amber with the rich depth of oud. The top notes of bergamot and green introduce a fresh and vibrant beginning, setting the tone for a scent experience that exudes luxury from the very first spritz. As the fragrance evolves, the heart reveals a decadent blend of melon, pineapple, gourmand, and amber. These middle notes create a sumptuous and inviting aroma that envelops you in a warm embrace, embodying the essence of refined elegance. The gourmand elements add a touch of sweetness, while the amber infuses the fragrance with a golden warmth that lingers on the skin. The journey concludes with the base notes of woody accords, vanilla, and musk, leaving an unforgettable, elegant trail that speaks of sophistication and taste. Presented in a gold-adorned bottle, Amber Oud Gold Edition is the epitome of luxury—a fragrance that is as memorable as it is captivating.  opulence and refinement.

REVIEW - AL HARAMAIN AMBER OUD (GOLD EDITION)

 What does it smell like? Playful-Fruity and colorful fresh summer scent that lasts a long long time. It's a clone of the very famous but much more expensive Sospiro Erba Pura.

 TOP NOTES - This one opens very fruity and fresh. The main fruits which I can detect are a freshly cut Melon backed by a smooth Pineapple note. All in all top notes are very very mouthwatering and tasty.

 MIDDLE - The Mid of this perfume has a bit more Vanilla and some extra sweetness to it. It gets very slight GREEN notes in this part of the composition but that adds nicely to the fruitiness of the perfume creating a good breathable fruity environment inside the scent.

 DRYDOWN - The base or the composition which arrives after a very very long time has some beautiful Amber and musk in it. Which leave a very smooth and intoxicating fragrance on my skin with some of the vanilla of the middle still left over.

 EMOTIONS - This is a very happy uplifting and playful,vibrant perfume. The artistry of this perfume is how it manages to stay fresh even without having any fresh notes.

 It's like the fruity notes are fresh and you're actually smelling freshly cut Melon and pineapple inside a garden on a beautiful summer day. Beautiful composition all in all.

 Performance - 12 Hours + on Skin

 24 Hours + on clothes.

 Projects very nicely for 3-4 hours. I've been asked about this scent after 6-7 hours of my initial wearing. Potent stuff but sometimes needs movement or some heat from the sun to really flow and then it provides a very good siliage.

 Usage - Men/Women over 18.

 Highly Recommended. Amazing packaging as well
