**Kismet Angel** by **Maison Alhambra** is a Oriental Vanilla fragrance for women and men. **Kismet Angel** was launched in 2020. Top notes are Vanilla, Honeycomb and Amber; middle notes are Cognac, Cinnamon, Caramel and Tonka Bean; base note is Dark Chocolate.
