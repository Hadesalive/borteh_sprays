**Bade'e Al Oud Sublime** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for women and men. **Bade'e Al Oud Sublime** was launched in 2023. Top notes are Apple, Litchi and Rose; middle notes are Plum and Jasmine; base notes are Moss, Vanilla and Patchouli.

Lattafa Badee Al Oud Sublime is a luxurious, woody fragrance with a hint of sweetness. It is a perfect blend of oud, sandalwood, and musk for a truly sublime experience.
