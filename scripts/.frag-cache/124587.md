**Jazzab Gold** by **Ard Al Zaafaran** is a Floral Fruity Gourmand fragrance for women. Top notes are Strawberry, Raspberry, Orange and Lemon; middle notes are Floral Notes, Rose, Spicy Notes and Cedarwood; base notes are Musk, Amber, Vanilla and Oud.
