**Hawas for Her** by **Rasasi** is a Floral fragrance for women. **Hawas for Her** was launched in 2015. Hawas for Her was created by Claire Liégent and Dominique Ropion. Top notes are Pomegranate, Apple and Grapefruit; middle notes are Iris, Jasmine Sambac and Citruses; base notes are Praline, Patchouli and Vetiver.
