**Salvo** by **Maison Alhambra** is a Oriental Fougere fragrance for men. **Salvo** was launched in 2022. Top note is Bergamot; middle notes are Lavender, Sichuan Pepper, Star Anise and Nutmeg; base notes are Ambroxan and Vanilla.
