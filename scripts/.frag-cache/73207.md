**Amber Oud Carbon Edition** by **Al Haramain Perfumes** is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for women and men. **Amber Oud Carbon Edition** was launched in 2022. Top notes are Bergamot, Lavender and Rosemary; middle notes are Sea Notes, Geranium and Sage; base notes are Moss, Vetiver, Amber and Cedar.
