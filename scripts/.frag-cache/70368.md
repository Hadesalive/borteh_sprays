Metallica10 06/16/26 15:19 

I have had this fragrance without using it, other than the initial spray, for at least 4 months. I decided to try it again. 

 The initial notes were screechy and difficult to get past. it was fruity, the apple and black currant, the rose mixed with some other floral, and the patchouli right up front. It does start to dry down, thank God, and becomes a little powdery, softened, but that rose is still prominent. I don't get any musk :( or sandalwood, another :( As I LOVE those notes in a dry down.
