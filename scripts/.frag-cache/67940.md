**Opulent Musk** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Oriental fragrance for women. **Opulent Musk** was launched in 2019. Top notes are White Musk, Saffron and Lemon; middle notes are White Flowers and Jasmine; base notes are White Musk, Cedar, Amber, Resin and Fir Resin.
