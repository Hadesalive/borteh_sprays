**Fakhar Rose** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Floral fragrance for women. **Fakhar Rose** was launched in 2022. Top notes are Fruits, Lily, Pomegranate and Aldehydes; middle notes are Tuberose, Jasmine, Gardenia, Ylang-Ylang, Rose, Honeysuckle and Peony; base notes are Vanilla, White Musk, Sandalwood and Ambroxan.
