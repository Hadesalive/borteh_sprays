**Eclaire** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Floral Fruity Gourmand fragrance for women. **Eclaire** was launched in 2024. Top notes are Caramel, Milk and Sugar; middle notes are Honey and White Flowers; base notes are Vanilla, Praline and Musk.
