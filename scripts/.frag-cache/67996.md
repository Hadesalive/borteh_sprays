**Qaed Al Fursan** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Oriental Woody fragrance for women and men. **Qaed Al Fursan** was launched in 2016. Top notes are Pineapple and Saffron; middle notes are Balsam Fir and Jasmine; base notes are Cedar, Amber and Agarwood (Oud).
