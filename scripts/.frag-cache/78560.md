**Nebras** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Oriental Vanilla fragrance for women and men. **Nebras** was launched in 2022. Top notes are Red Berries and Mandarin Orange; middle notes are Vanilla, Cacao and Rose; base notes are Sugar, Tonka Bean, Amber and Musk.
