**Edge** by **Swiss Arabian** is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for men. Top notes are Lavender, Mint, Geranium and Lemon; middle notes are Orchid, Rose, Cardamom and Cinnamon; base notes are Sandalwood, Cedar and White Musk.

"Add an edge to your personality. Edge Men is the symbolic aroma of the modern man – charismatic, daring and powerful. Let this edgy, audacious fragrance envelop you in a distinctly masculine aura that captivates your mood and style." - a note from the brand.
