**Yara Tous** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a fragrance for women. **Yara Tous** was launched in 2023. Top notes are Mango, Coconut and Passionfruit; middle notes are Jasmine, Orange Blossom and Heliotrope; base notes are Vanilla, Musk and Cashmeran.
