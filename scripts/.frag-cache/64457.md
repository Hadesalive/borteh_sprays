**Bucephalus XI** by **Armaf** is a Oriental Spicy fragrance for women and men. Top notes are Black Currant, Pineapple and Bergamot; middle notes are Myrrh, Jasmine and Rose; base notes are Vanilla, Amber, Oakmoss and Musk.
