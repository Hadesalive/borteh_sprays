**Barakkat Rouge 540 Extrait de Parfum** by **Fragrance World** is a Oriental Woody fragrance for women and men. **Barakkat Rouge 540 Extrait de Parfum** was launched in 2024. Top notes are Bitter Almond and Saffron; middle notes are Cedar and Jasmine; base notes are Woody Notes, Ambergris and Musk.

A woody extrait de parfum inspired by Maison Francis Kurkdjian's Baccarat Rouge 540, featuring a blend of bitter almond, saffron, jasmine, and musky woody accords. The fragrance emphasizes a fusion of elements tamed by perfumery artistry, housed in a bottle with a distinctive red aesthetic.
