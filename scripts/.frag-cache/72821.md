**Asad** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Oriental fragrance for men. **Asad** was launched in 2021. Top notes are Black Pepper, Tobacco and Pineapple; middle notes are Patchouli, Coffee and Iris; base notes are Vanilla, Amber, Dry Wood, Benzoin and Labdanum.
