**Qaed Al Fursan Unlimited** by **Lattafa Perfumes** is a Floral Fruity Gourmand fragrance for women and men. **Qaed Al Fursan Unlimited** was launched in 2022. Top notes are Coconut, Pineapple and Citruses; middle notes are Ylang-Ylang, Frangipani and Jasmine; base notes are Vanilla, Musk, Sandalwood and Sweet Notes.
