**Club De Nuit Urban Elixir** by **Armaf** is a fragrance for men. **Club De Nuit Urban Elixir** was launched in 2022. Top notes are Bergamot, Pink Pepper, Orange Blossom and Jasmine; middle notes are Lavender, elemi, Geranium, Vetiver, Saffron and Marigold; base notes are Ambroxan, Amber, Cedar, Patchouli and Labdanum.
