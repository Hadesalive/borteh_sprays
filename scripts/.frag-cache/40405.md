**L'Aventure** by **Al Haramain Perfumes** is a Chypre Fruity fragrance for men. **L'Aventure** was launched in 2016. Top notes are Lemon, Bergamot and elemi; middle notes are Woody Notes, Jasmine and Lily-of-the-Valley; base notes are Musk, Patchouli and Amber.
